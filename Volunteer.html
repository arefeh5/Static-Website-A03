Join Our Volunteer Program

Volunteering with the Beacon of Wellness Alliance (BOWA) is a unique opportunity to make a tangible difference in the lives of youth and under-served communities. Whether you're interested in helping with health initiatives, organizing local events, or mentoring adolescents, BOWA offers a variety of ways for you to contribute.

Why Volunteer with BOWA?

By volunteering with BOWA, you become a part of a compassionate community that focuses on:

  • Empowering young individuals to make informed choices about their health and wellness.
  • Providing mentorship and support through mental health and social awareness programs.
  • Collaborating on educational initiatives that create lasting impacts in the community.

How to Get Involved

We have many opportunities available for those looking to contribute their time and skills:

  • Community Outreach
  • Event Planning and Fundraising
  • Mental Health Mentorship
  • Health Awareness Campaigns
